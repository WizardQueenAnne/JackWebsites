Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about my web design services.

How long does it take to build a website?

Most projects are completed within 1-3 days depending on the complexity and package selected. If you need your website even faster, I offer a 24-hour rush delivery option for an additional 25% of the package price.

Do I need to provide content for my website?

Yes, you'll need to provide the basic content (text and images) for your website. I can help with basic content organization and suggestions, but you know your business best! I'll help you organize everything to look great.

How do domain names and hosting work?

You'll need to purchase a domain name (your website address, like yourname.com) and hosting (where your website lives online). I'll recommend affordable options based on your needs and help you set everything up. You'll own these accounts and pay for them directly (typically $12-15/year for a domain and $5-10/month for hosting).

Can I update the website myself after it's built?

Yes! I build websites that are easy to update, and I'll provide basic training on how to make simple changes to your content. For more complex updates, you can either add the monthly maintenance plan or contact me for specific changes as needed.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept payment via PayPal, Venmo, or direct bank transfer. For all projects, I require a 50% deposit before starting, with the remaining 50% due upon completion before the website goes live.

How do we get started?

Simply fill out the contact form below with some details about your project, and I'll get back to you within 24 hours to schedule a consultation call. Once we've discussed your needs, I'll provide a detailed proposal for your approval.
